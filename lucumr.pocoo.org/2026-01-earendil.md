# Colin and Earendil

> Armin Ronacher announces the founding of Earendil, a new company co-founded with Colin — a San Francisco native he met in Vienna.

## Key Facts
- Earendil is a Public Benefit Corporation with an explicit mission around human agency and open protocols, not just commercial success.
- Ronacher frames the venture as a product of his open source background and a response to AI reshaping the software profession.
- Vienna is positioned as a deliberate choice of base — offering cultural distance from Silicon Valley and a tradition of independent-minded builders.
- The founders' stated goal is to "leave the world a better place than we found it," with earnestness treated as a core operating principle.

## Summary
Armin Ronacher announces the founding of Earendil, a new company co-founded with Colin — a San Francisco native he met in Vienna. The post reflects on Ronacher's two decades in open source and the disruptive impact of AI on the software profession. Earendil is incorporated as a Public Benefit Corporation with a charter centred on creating software and open protocols, strengthening human agency, bridging division, and cultivating understanding. Rather than resisting technological change, the founders aim to steer it toward positive outcomes, grounded in the values they associate with Vienna's culture of tinkerers and troublemakers outside the Silicon Valley bubble.
