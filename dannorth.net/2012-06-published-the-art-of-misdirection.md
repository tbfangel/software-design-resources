# Published: The art of misdirection

> North announced publication of "The Art of Misdirection" in The Developer magazine ahead of NDC 2012 conference in Oslo.

## Key Facts
- Opportunity cost framework applies to software development practices and trade-offs
- Publishing articles ahead of conference talks amplifies impact and engagement
- Trade-offs and choices fundamentally impact development effectiveness
- Community engagement through comments enhances article value

## Summary
North announced publication of "The Art of Misdirection" in The Developer magazine ahead of NDC 2012 conference in Oslo. The article explores opportunity cost, examining how choices and trade-offs impact decision-making and effectiveness. The piece was published to coincide with the "excellent NDC 2012 event," making it timely for the software development conference audience. The full article is available on North's website.
