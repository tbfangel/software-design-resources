# Better Best Practices at ExpertZone Stockholm

> North announced speaking at ExpertZone Developer Summit in Stockholm, presenting on best practices within the "People Matters Too" track.

## Key Facts
- Best practices discussions benefit from non-technical framing focused on human learning
- Understanding why people rebel against change programmes is essential for effective adoption
- DDD and BDD intersect in meaningful ways worth exploring
- Combining people-focused and technical talks addresses different audience needs

## Summary
North announced speaking at ExpertZone Developer Summit in Stockholm, presenting on best practices within the "People Matters Too" track. He describes it as a "completely non-technical" discussion examining "how we learn and why we rebel when faced with Yet Another Change Programme." The same trip included a presentation at Stockholm Javaforum addressing the intersection of Domain-Driven Design and Behaviour-Driven Development, with approximately 200 attendees. The timing aligned with his work on an article exploring the relationship between these methodologies.
