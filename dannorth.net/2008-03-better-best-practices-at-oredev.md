# Better Best Practices at Øredev

> North delivered a keynote at Øredev conference in Malmö, Sweden, stepping in for testing guru James Bach, alongside Joel Spolsky and Andy Hunt.

## Key Facts
- "Best practices" deserves rigorous deconstruction rather than uncritical acceptance
- Questioning conventional wisdom is central to improving professional practice
- Understanding opposing perspectives strengthens arguments
- High-profile conference platforms enable reaching broader audiences with challenging ideas

## Summary
North delivered a keynote at Øredev conference in Malmö, Sweden, stepping in for testing guru James Bach, alongside Joel Spolsky and Andy Hunt. The talk addressed best practices in software development, exploring themes around rigorously deconstructing the phrase "best practices" and picking apart arguments of those who accept them uncritically. North notes that Bach "rigorously deconstructs the phrase" and "picks apart the arguments" of those who disagree with his perspective. The talk was published as an article on InfoQ and recorded as video. North later discovered he had unknowingly referenced an article by Bach himself during the talk.
